# Data Provenance — D2C Guardrails Engine

## Overview
This project uses a mix of real and synthesized data. Every brand gets its own provenance file at `data/{brand}/data_provenance.md` generated automatically by `ingest.js`.

---

## Mamaearth (Primary Brand)

### Social Layer (`data/mamaearth/social.json`)
- **Status:** Synthesized
- **Grounded in:** HONASA Consumer public Instagram presence (~600K followers as of FY23)
- **Generated by:** `prepare_mamaearth.js` from raw `datasets/honasa_social.json`

### Sales Layer (`data/mamaearth/sales.json`)
- **Status:** Synthesized
- **Grounded in:** HONASA FY23 annual revenue ₹1,492 Cr, product portfolio from public filings
- **Real SKUs used:** Onion Hair Oil 200ml, Vitamin C Face Wash, Rosemary Shampoo, Rice Water Face Wash, 10% Niacinamide Serum, Sunscreen SPF 50
- **Generated by:** `prepare_mamaearth.js` from raw `datasets/honasa_sales.json`

### Customer Layer (`data/mamaearth/customers.json`)
- **Status:** Synthesized
- **Grounded in:** Publicly observable Amazon India review patterns, known complaint categories per product type
- **Generated by:** `prepare_mamaearth.js` from raw `datasets/honasa_customers.json`

### Ads Layer (`data/mamaearth/ads.json`)
- **Status:** Synthesized
- **Grounded in:** HONASA disclosed marketing spend ~32% of revenue (FY23)
- **Generated by:** `prepare_mamaearth.js` from raw `datasets/honosa_marketing.json`

---

## Other Brands (Nykaa, Starbucks, etc.)

- **Status:** Fully synthesized by GPT-4o at runtime
- **Grounded in:** Brand name + public knowledge only
- **Not anchored to public filings**
- **Generated by:** `server.js → generateBrandData()` when brand is first requested
